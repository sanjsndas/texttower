Your Rights: Access, Rectification, and Erasure of Personal Data

At TextTowernaxi, we are firm believers in transparent data practices concerning the personal data we gather from you.   • Right to Erasure (Deletion): In compliance with applicable laws, such as the California Consumer Privacy Act, you possess the right to request the deletion of your personal data from our records. We will assess your request and, where legally permissible, securely remove your data. Please note that certain legal obligations may require us to retain specific information for a defined period.
  • Right to Opt-out: You may also choose to opt-out of receiving non-essential communications from us. Such opt-out requests will be processed promptly, although certain transactional communications necessary for service operation may still be sent.

To initiate requests for access, correction, or deletion of your personal data, please follow the instructions available through our dedicated service portal. We strive to process all requests expeditiously and in accordance with relevant laws. Our commitment is to foster confidence in our data management practices and empower you with control over your personal information.
